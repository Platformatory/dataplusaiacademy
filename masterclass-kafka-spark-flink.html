About this Masterclass

Real-world streaming,
taught by practitioners.

Avinash Upadhyaya and Pavan Keshavamurthy are kicking off a series of masterclass-style workshops on Apache Kafka, Spark and Flink as part of Data+AI Academy. This is an intermediate to advanced track — built for those who want to go beyond tutorials and truly understand data engineering and streaming at depth.

Curriculum

What we'll cover

Essential Data Infra Theory

Data(bases) and The Log — the internals of why things work the way they do. Build the mental models that separate engineers who operate systems from those who understand them.

Deep-Dive into Data Processing

From Producers and Consumers all the way up to full data processing frameworks. Kafka internals, Spark execution model, Flink stateful streaming — covered properly.

Practical Use-Cases & Hands-on Labs

Industry use-cases across all three frameworks with follow-along labs. You'll leave having actually built things, not just watched slides.

Certification Guidance

For those who want it — structured guidance towards Confluent and Databricks certifications to formalise what you learn.
