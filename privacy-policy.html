Privacy Policy

We value your trust. Learn how we collect, safeguard, and use your personal information.

Last updated: January 13, 2026

DataPlus AI Academy ("we", "us", "our") operates the website https://dataplusai.academy/ (the "Platform"), providing online courses, bootcamps, learning resources, certifications, newsletters and related services in the field of Data, AI, Machine Learning and associated technologies.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Platform, register for an account, enroll in a course, make a purchase, or interact with our services.

01 Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing/shipping information (when you purchase courses/subscriptions)
  • Profile information (photo, bio, job title, LinkedIn etc.)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-parties)

b) Automatically Collected

  • IP address, browser type/version, device information
  • Operating system, referring/exit pages, timestamps
  • Pages viewed, time spent, clickstream data
  • Cookies, web beacons, pixels

c) Information from Third Parties: Login via Google/LinkedIn (if implemented), Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe, etc.), Analytics providers (Google Analytics, etc.).

02 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain and improve our courses and Platform

  • Process enrollments, payments and issue certificates

  • Send important service emails (enrollment confirmations, course updates, password resets)

  • Send marketing communications/newsletters (you can opt-out anytime)

  • Personalize your learning experience and recommendations

  • Detect, prevent and address technical issues, fraud or abuse

  • Comply with legal obligations

  • Conduct research & analytics (aggregated/anonymized)

03 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential functionality, analytics, and (if applicable) advertising. You can manage preferences through your browser settings.

04 Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers (hosting, email service, payment processors, LMS platforms, cloud storage, etc.) — under strict confidentiality
  • Business partners (only with your consent — e.g. job placement partners)
  • In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • To comply with law, respond to legal requests, protect rights/safety

05 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

06 Your Rights

Access data Correct/Update Delete personal data Object/Restrict processing Withdraw consent

Depending on your location you may have these rights. To exercise these rights, contact us at enroll@dataplusai.academy

07 International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in India and other countries. We use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

08 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on the Platform.
